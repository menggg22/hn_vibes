# HN Vibes

Hacker News reflects the pulse of the builder community better than almost anywhere else. It’s a real-time signal of what deeply technical, world-class builders actually care about.

So that’s the purpose of this log: to track that pulse every day, the mood, the debates, the fresh Show HNs, and the ideas gaining traction as AI reshapes what’s possible. It points to what’s ahead. And, over time, it should also serve as a record of how it all unfolded.

Feel free to browse through it, or follow along, to see what’s new today.

**[→ Browse today's entries](https://github.com/menggg22/hn_vibes/tree/main/vibes/2026/daily)**

The front page score shows what’s popular. The builder pulse shows what’s being built right now. The classification table lets you scan across weeks and spot patterns—what keeps resurfacing, what’s rising, and what’s fading. The Show HN fresh/momentum split is the key insight: a Show HN with 900pts posted 3 days ago tells you nothing about today's builder energy. A Show HN with 30pts posted 2 hours ago tells you what someone decided to ship tonight.

## What's in each entry

Every day at 6pm Pacific, the log captures:

**Top of the file** — quick read:
- **Top themes** — what dominated today
- **Vibe** — mood in 1-2 sentences
- **Standout** — the one story worth clicking
- **Show HN lesson** — what the best Show HN today did right (or wrong)

**Summary section** — the useful part:
- **AI mood** — excited / skeptical / fatigued / anxious, with evidence
- **Energy leaders** — top stories by score × comments (not just score)
- **Hot debates** — high comment/score ratio, where people are actually arguing
- **Builder pulse** — Show HNs posted in the last 24h, grouped by theme. What are builders shipping *right now*?
- **Show HN momentum** — older posts still climbing
- **Idea gaps** — Ask HN questions with traction, unsolved problems
- **Suggested deep dives** — 2-3 links worth your time today, with a specific reason why

**Personal Signal** — ml-infra / on-device ML / agent-systems filter:
- **ml-infra & on-device ML** — trend + links for ML infrastructure, on-device inference, model serving, fine-tuning
- **agent-systems** — trend + links for agent orchestration, memory, tooling, multi-agent systems

**Classification table** — every story categorized:

| Title | pts | comments | category | source | fresh |
|-------|-----|----------|----------|--------|-------|

Categories: `llm-release`, `agent-systems`, `ml-infra`, `security`, `infra`, `tools`, `hardware`, `science`, `energy`, `geopolitics`, `culture`, `learning`

Sources: `front` (front page), `show` (Show HN), `ask` (Ask HN)

Fresh: `yes` if posted in the last 24h

## How it's generated

Fetches 60 front page + 30 Show HN + 20 Ask HN stories daily via the [HN Firebase API](https://github.com/HackerNews/API), in parallel batches. Editorial summary generated by Claude.

Part of [menggg22/menggg_hub](https://github.com/menggg22/personal_knowlege_os.git) — personal knowledge OS.
