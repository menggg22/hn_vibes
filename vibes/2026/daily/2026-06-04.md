The ESP32-S31 appearing at 240pts suggests the embedded/on-device ML community is watching new silicon closely.

- [Gemma 4 12B: A unified, encoder-free multimodal model](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48385906) — 637pts — encoder-free architecture is the architectural bet worth understanding; what does it give up and what does it unlock for edge deployment?

**agent-systems**: The pricing and access-control layer around agents is becoming the real engineering problem — not the agents themselves. Both the Uber AI pricing debate and the access-control Show HN point to the same gap: agents acting with real permissions in real systems need governance primitives that don't yet exist.

- [Uber's $1,500/month AI limit is a useful signal for AI tool pricing](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48383056) — 337pts — the comment thread is effectively a live market research session on what enterprises will actually pay for agent productivity; read the comments
