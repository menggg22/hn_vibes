# HN Vibes — 2026-05-11

**Top themes**: AI sovereignty, hardware lock-in, open-source defiance, infrastructure revolt, builder nostalgia

**Vibe**: HN is in a combative mood today — angry at monopoly capture (Google reCAPTCHA, AWS, hardware attestation) and rallying hard around local AI, open archives, and low-level craftsmanship as acts of resistance. The "build it yourself or lose it" ethos is palpable across the front page.

**Standout**: [Google broke reCAPTCHA for de-googled Android users](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48067119) — the single highest-scoring story of the day at 1513pts with 564 comments; a concrete, reproducible case of a platform weaponizing friction against users who opted out, which hits HN's anti-lock-in nerve precisely.

**Show HN lesson**: [Building a web server in assembly to give my life (a lack of) meaning](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48080587) earned 386pts by leading with self-deprecating absurdity in the title — the joke signals "this is real craft, not a product pitch," which disarms skepticism and invites curiosity instantly.
