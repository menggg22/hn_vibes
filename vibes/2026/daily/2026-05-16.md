## 🔭 Depth — ml-infra / on-device ML / agent-systems

*What's the trend and what sparks curiosity.*

**ml-infra & on-device ML**: The on-device inference story is quietly maturing — distillation to tiny models (Needle's 26M tool-calling model) and local-first ML tools are appearing without fanfare, suggesting the "runs on your machine" pitch is becoming table stakes rather than a differentiator. Watch for the next wave to compete on latency and battery, not just parameter count.
- [Show HN: Needle: We Distilled Gemini Tool Calling into a 26M Model](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48111896) — 747pts — the interesting question isn't the distillation, it's whether 26M is a floor or a ceiling for reliable tool-calling

**agent-systems**: MCP ecosystem tooling is embryonic but accelerating — an open-source package manager for AI skills (Sx) appearing as a Show HN suggests the community is trying to impose order on a fast-fragmenting agent-tool landscape before it becomes unmaintainable. The Amazon/AI-task-fabrication story is the dark mirror of this: agents in enterprise without accountability structure produce Potemkin productivity.
- [Show HN: Sx – an open-source package manager for AI skills, MCPs, and commands](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48151058) — 31pts — if MCP is the plugin model, this is the app store; worth watching whether it gains a package registry before a VC-backed competitor does
