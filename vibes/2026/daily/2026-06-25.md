*What's the trend and what sparks curiosity.*

**ml-infra & on-device ML**: Custom silicon is moving from hyperscaler exclusive to AI-lab standard — OpenAI/Broadcom joining Apple/Google/Amazon signals that inference cost control is now existential, not optional. Meanwhile the 45°C data center cooling story suggests the physical plant is under serious strain from inference demand.
- [45°C cooling design cuts data center water use to near zero](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48660178) — 144pts — if this scales, it decouples GPU density from water rights, which is currently a hard constraint on where you can build inference clusters

GLM-5.2 and Qwen-AgentWorld suggest open-weights alternatives are narrowing the gap on agentic benchmarks faster than expected.
- [Qwen-AgentWorld: Language World Models for General Agents](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48654351) — 194pts — world-model-backed agents rather than pure reactive chains; if the evals hold, this is a different architectural bet worth watching
