## 🔭 Depth — ml-infra / on-device ML / agent-systems

*What's the trend and what sparks curiosity.*

**ml-infra & on-device ML**: On-device inference crossed a visible threshold today — Gemma 4 running full offline on an iPhone without cloud calls is the headline, and the JIT retrofitting piece underneath it asks whether the C interpreter ecosystem is the next target for compiler speedups.
