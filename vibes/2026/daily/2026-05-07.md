## 🔭 Depth — ml-infra / on-device ML / agent-systems

*What's the trend and what sparks curiosity.*

**ml-infra & on-device ML**: Inference optimization is moving fast enough that Google is shipping multi-token prediction drafters for Gemma 4 while simultaneously pushing on-device models through Chrome without asking — two very different deployment philosophies colliding publicly today. The on-device story is fracturing between "empowering" and "extractive" depending entirely on consent mechanics.
- [Accelerating Gemma 4: faster inference with multi-token prediction drafters](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48024540) — 656pts — speculative decoding via learned draft heads is becoming the standard inference speed-up playbook; worth understanding the architecture tradeoffs

**agent-systems**: The line between "agent does useful work" and "agent takes irreversible real-world financial actions" just got crossed in a visible, public way with Cloudflare. The Tilde.run transactional filesystem and the Claude/SpaceX compute deal both signal that the infrastructure layer for accountable agents is being built in real time, under competitive pressure.
- [Agents can now create Cloudflare accounts, buy domains, and deploy](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48031684) — 618pts — the specific question worth reading the thread for: what does "undo" mean when an agent has already paid for something?
- [Higher usage limits for Claude and a compute deal with SpaceX](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48037986) — 350pts — Anthropic trading compute capacity for usage headroom is a new business model pattern worth tracking

**Deep dives**:
