## 🔭 Depth — ml-infra / on-device ML / agent-systems

*What's the trend and what sparks curiosity.*

**ml-infra & on-device ML**: The open-model frontier is compressing fast — Kimi K3 and the German Soofi S 30B both land today, and LM Studio Bionic is positioning local inference as the agent runtime of choice. The gap between frontier closed and frontier open is the conversation.
- [Kimi K3: Open Frontier Intelligence](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48935342) — 1053pts — worth reading the comments specifically for the benchmark credibility debate; 656 comments is a signal that people are genuinely stress-testing the claims

- [Agent-talk: Enabling coding agents to work together](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48936534) — 42pts — multi-agent coordination protocol; the interesting question buried here is who arbitrates when agents disagree
- [Schema Harness Achieves ~99% on ARC-AGI-3 Public](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48935905) — 84pts — 99% on ARC-AGI-3 would have been front-page-dominating news 18 months ago; today it's mid-pack, which is its own data point about benchmark inflation

**Deep dives**:
