## 🔭 Depth — ml-infra / on-device ML / agent-systems

*What's the trend and what sparks curiosity.*

**ml-infra & on-device ML**: Tokenizer economics are becoming a first-class engineering concern — the Claude 4.7 cost measurement post is a sign that teams are now treating token budgets the way they once treated database query plans. The "are AI agent costs rising exponentially?" piece from 2025 resurfacing suggests the community hasn't resolved the unit-economics question yet.
- [Measuring Claude 4.7's tokenizer costs](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47807006) — 518pts — worth reading for the methodology: if someone has built a reproducible tokenizer cost benchmark, that framework will outlast the specific model

**agent-systems**: Codex is the headline, but the more interesting signal is builders wiring agents into physical-world feedback loops (SPICE/oscilloscope) and financial data streams (LangAlpha, SEC filings). The question is moving from "can agents do tasks?" to "which environments make agents reliable enough to trust?"
- [Codex for almost everything](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47796469) — 985pts — the "almost" in the title is doing enormous work; read the comments to map where practitioners say it breaks down

**Deep dives**:
- [Ada, its design, and the language that built the languages](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47803844) — 246pts, 170c — with safety-critical software back in the conversation (avionics, autonomous systems), Ada's design philosophy is historically instructive and practically relevant right now
- [Reflections on 30 years of HPC programming](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47759436) — 135pts, 105c — a longitudinal view of what actually lasted in high-performance computing is exactly the corrective lens needed when everyone is chasing the new hotness
- [Human Accelerated Region 1](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47802112) — 114pts — the genomics angle connects to the "landmark ancient-genome study shows surprise acceleration of human evolution" story also trending today; two data points make a theme worth investigating
